Professional Interests

At Cal Poly I teach Digital Printing and Variable Data Publishing, Content Management and Web Design, and the first phase of the Senior Project.

I have more than 30 years' experience in commercial, newspaper, and digital printing operations including serving as manager for regional operations of USA Today. Previously, I worked for Xerox Corporation, developing patented image analysis techniques. I'm a graduate of the Rochester Institute of Technology School of Print Media.

My published research includes work in digital printing, personalized communication, and the news media industry. At Cal Poly led a student driven project testing Adobe's PDF/VT file format, and have recently completed a study on how demographic factors affect purchasing decisions in food packaging.
