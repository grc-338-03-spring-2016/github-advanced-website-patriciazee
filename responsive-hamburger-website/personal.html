Personal Interests

I have a number of interests outside of graphic communication including science, history and economics. I might take my telescope out on a dark evening, or just grab a good book. Hiking is one of my favorite pastimes. It's a good way to exercise and I tend to get some of my best ideas on the trails. And when I really need to waste some down time I'll watch an old sci fi movie. In fact, I pride myself as an expert on mid-twenth century science fiction. In the past, I painted watercolor and was a pretty good amateur photographer. When I have more time I'm sure I'll be getting back to those pursuits.
